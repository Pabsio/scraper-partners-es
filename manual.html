Enrichment Agent · Curator

Find the best flight and hotel deals, select them, and copy HTML snippets straight into Contentful.

01 · Introduction

What is the Curator?

The Enrichment Agent Curator is an internal tool for the HolidayPirates editorial team. It connects directly to Google Sheets where the enrichment agent deposits flight and hotel data, and lets you filter, sort, select and copy HTML snippets ready to paste into Contentful.

In one sentence: you find the best deal, check the price, and copy the snippet — all in one place.

What you can do

  • Flights: filter by market, origin, destination, month, day, nights and price. Select deals and copy their HTML snippets into Contentful.
  • Hotels: filter hotel dates by market, hotel name, month, day, nights and price per night. Check prices via Trivago. Copying snippets to Contentful is coming soon.

The tool lives at enrichment-agents.netlify.app — open it directly in your browser, no setup needed.

ℹ️ The tool connects to Google Sheets automatically on load. If data doesn't appear, check your internet connection and try the ↺ Reload button.
02 · Quick start

Your first selection in 60 seconds

If you only read one section, read this one.

Open the tool — go to enrichment-agents.netlify.app. Flights for the ES market load automatically.
Pick your market — click a market chip (🇪🇸 ES, 🇩🇪 DE, 🇬🇧 UK…). The table reloads with that market's data.
Apply filters — narrow down by origin, destination, month, day of week, nights or price. Filters update the results instantly.
Select deals — check the boxes on the rows you want, or click ☰ Top 20 cheapest to auto-select the best ones.
Copy snippets — click ↓ Copy selected. All HTML snippets are copied to your clipboard, one after another.
Paste into Contentful — paste directly. Done.

04 · Flights

Flights — Market selection

Click a market chip to load flights for that market. Data is cached per session — switching between markets and back does not reload until you hit ↺ Reload.

🇪🇸 ES 🇩🇪 DE 🇫🇷 FR 🇮🇹 IT 🇬🇧 UK 🇳🇱 NL 🇵🇱 PL 🇨🇭 CH 🇺🇸 US 🇦🇹 AT

Markets shown in grey (NL, PL, CH, US, AT) have not been connected yet and will be activated as flight data becomes available.

Flights — Filters

Origin & Destination

These two chip groups are dynamic: selecting an origin shows only the destinations available from that origin, and vice versa. Select All to reset.

Departure month & Departure day

Both support multi-selection — click individual chips to toggle them on or off. Clicking All deselects everything else. Useful for comparing weekends across two months, for example.

Nights

Double-handle slider. Drag the left handle to set the minimum and the right handle to set the maximum. You can also type values directly in the number inputs — both controls stay in sync.

Max price (round trip)

Single-handle slider. Drag left to lower the maximum price. Shows No limit at the far right. You can also type in the input field.

Flights — Results table

Results are sorted by price ascending by default. Click any sortable column header to change the sort:

ColumnPrimary sortTiebreaker order
DepartureDatePrice → Nights
NightsNightsDate → Price
PricePriceDate → Nights

Click the same column again to reverse the sort order (↑ ascending / ↓ descending).

🔥 Flame badge

Appears on the cheapest flight in the current filtered results that is also flagged as a minimum price in the source data. Use it as a quick signal for the most attractive deal.

Check ↗ link

Opens the flight directly on Skyscanner or the partner site so you can verify the current price before publishing. Always check before committing to a deal.

ℹ️ Prices sourced from Google Flights. Fares are indicative and may differ slightly on Skyscanner or Kayak due to real-time availability. Always verify via the Check ↗ link before publishing.

Flights — Copying snippets to Contentful

Once you've filtered and found the deals you want:

Check the box on each row you want to include. Selected rows highlight in purple.
Or click ☰ Top 20 cheapest to automatically select the 20 cheapest flights in the current filtered set.
Click ↓ Copy N snippets. All HTML snippets are copied to your clipboard, one after another, in the current sort order.
Paste directly into Contentful.

To clear your selection, click ✕ Deselect all.

05 · Hotels

Hotels — Market selection

All 10 markets are available for hotels. Click a chip to load that market's hotel data. ES loads automatically when you open the Hotels tab.

🇪🇸 ES 🇩🇪 DE 🇬🇧 UK 🇫🇷 FR 🇮🇹 IT 🇳🇱 NL 🇵🇱 PL 🇨🇭 CH 🇺🇸 US 🇦🇹 AT

Data is cached per market during the session. Switching markets and switching back does not make a new request until you reload.

ℹ️ Prices extracted from Google Hotels — used as a reference to identify the cheapest check-in dates. When you check the price on Trivago it may turn out to be even cheaper.

Hotels — Filters

Hotel

Select a specific hotel from the chips to see only its dates. Select All to show dates across all hotels in the market.

Month & Check-in day

Both support multi-selection, same as in the Flights tab. Toggle individual chips on/off; All resets the filter.

Nights

Double-handle slider with number inputs, same as Flights.

Max price per night

Single-handle slider. The filter applies to the per-night price, not the total stay.

🔥 Flame badge

Appears independently on two cells: the cheapest price/night and the cheapest total in the current filtered results. Both are highlighted separately, so the cheapest per-night date and the cheapest total stay may be different rows.

Sorting

You can sort by Check-in, Nights, ~Price/night or ~Total. Click a column header to sort; click again to reverse.

06 · Reference

Workflow tips

Recommended flight workflow

  1. Select the market you're working on.
  2. Filter by destination (or origin if relevant).
  3. Apply month and day-of-week filters to match your editorial calendar.
  4. Click ☰ Top 20 cheapest to pre-select the best deals.
  5. Review and adjust the selection manually if needed.
  6. Click ↓ Copy selected and paste into Contentful.

Multi-select filters

Month and day chips support multiple selection. Useful for comparing weekends across two months: select Sat Sun and Jun 26 Jul 26 simultaneously.

Reload & cache

Data is cached per market during the session. If the Google Sheet has been updated since you opened the tool, click the ↺ Reload button (top right) to refresh all data for both tabs.

Verifying prices

  • Flights: prices are round-trip fares from Google Flights. May vary slightly on Skyscanner or Kayak.
  • Hotels: prices are per-night approximations from Google Hotels. The actual Trivago price may be lower.
  • Always verify via the Check ↗ link before publishing.

Dynamic origin / destination chips

When you select an origin, the destination chips automatically update to show only the airports that have flights from that origin — and vice versa. This prevents empty result sets and makes it faster to navigate to a specific route.

Upcoming features

  • Hotel snippets for Contentful: copy HTML snippets for hotel deals — same workflow as flights.
  • More markets for flights: NL, PL, CH, US, AT flight data will be connected as enrichment is activated for those markets.
  • Outlet tab: a historical archive of the cheapest deals found over time, for outlet-style content.
